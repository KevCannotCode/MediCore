Services

Primary Care

Our primary care services focus on preventive measures, early diagnosis, and ongoing management of chronic conditions. We offer annual physicals, vaccinations, screenings, and wellness consultations to help you stay on top of your health

Specialized Treatments

MediCore has a team of specialists across various fields, including cardiology, orthopedics, neurology, oncology, and more. We provide expert care for complex medical issues with personalized treatment plans for each patient.

Emergency & Urgent Care

When immediate care is needed, MediCore’s emergency and urgent care units are ready to help. Our facility is staffed 24/7 by emergency care experts who provide prompt and effective treatment.

Diagnostic Imaging & Labs

With cutting-edge imaging equipment and an on-site laboratory, MediCore offers fast, accurate diagnostics. From MRI and CT scans to blood work and biopsies, we have everything needed to support your diagnosis and treatment.

Telemedicine & Virtual Consultations

We understand that healthcare should be accessible and convenient. Our telemedicine services allow patients to consult with their doctors from the comfort of their homes, ensuring continuity of care.

Surgical Services

fully equipped surgical suites and highly trained surgical team ensure safe and effective procedures, from minimally invasive surgeries to more complex operations
